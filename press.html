DRIFTRAGE is a neon-wasteland arcade driver where your car is the weapon. Drift into enemy waves, survive a shrinking storm, and build your wreck into a beast across runs. Six enemy archetypes — chase, lunge, kite, joust, anchor, and air — each demand a different drift. Seeded daily runs, shareable clips, and a wreck-to-beast progression keep every session worth one more try.
